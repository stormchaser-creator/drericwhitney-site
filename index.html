Clinical Practice

I am a board-certified neurosurgeon practicing with Arrowhead Neurosurgical Medical Group across Southern California's Inland Empire and Coachella Valley. My hospital affiliations include Eisenhower Health, Arrowhead Regional Medical Center, Riverside University Health System, and Desert Regional Medical Center.

I completed my neurosurgical residency at Arrowhead Regional Medical Center and my internship at Desert Regional Medical Center. I graduated from Liberty University College of Osteopathic Medicine and hold board certification from the American Osteopathic Board of Surgery in Neurosurgery.

My clinical work spans the full spectrum of neurosurgery: trauma, spine, cerebrovascular disease, and neuro-oncology.

Research Interests

My research explores the intersection of clinical neurosurgery and the science of consciousness. Published and ongoing work includes traumatic brain injury, cerebral aneurysms, the neurological effects of music and binaural beats, and a soliton-based framework for understanding consciousness loss in aneurysmal subarachnoid hemorrhage.

I am particularly interested in how neurosurgical observations can inform our understanding of consciousness — what we lose, what we recover, and what that tells us about the architecture of the mind.

Projects

The Decoded Human

An AI-powered literature connectome that discovers hidden semantic connections across open-access scientific papers. We ingest research from PMC, bioRxiv, and medRxiv, extract core scientific content, and build a living knowledge graph where papers are connected not just by citations, but by what they actually say.

Still You Recovery

Free resources for the emotional, cognitive, and identity changes that nobody tells you about after brain surgery or stroke. Written because the emotional aftermath of neurological recovery is one of the most consistently underexplained aspects of patient care. No registration. No paywalls.

ANMG CallSync

A scheduling platform for multi-site neuroscience on-call coverage, designed to coordinate provider networks across multiple hospitals and clinical sites.

Writing
